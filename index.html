Create A Bootable USB Drive.

A simple guide to creating and using a bootable USB drive.

Get Started

Why?

There are many reasons to create a bootable USB drive. For example, if your computer crashes and becomes unusable, then a bootable USB drive has the power to reinstall the entire operating system.

How?

It depends on what operating system you are using.

What Do I Need?

You'll need a few things to get started:

  • A USB Flash Drive, preferably 4 GB or more
  • A computer with an internet connection
